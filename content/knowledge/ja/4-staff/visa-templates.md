## 📝 ビザ用テンプレートの使い方  
以下のテキストブロックには、日本の出入国在留管理局での審査に使用する、承認済みの公式な英文テンプレートを掲載しています。これは、あなたの宗教活動としての立場、派遣元組織による支援、そして海外からの経済的支援によって生活が成り立っていることを示すための文面です。派遣元の教会、教団、またはミッションネットワークに対して、この文章をそのまま公式レターヘッドにコピーし、角括弧 `[ ]` 内の項目を正しく記入したうえで、直筆の署名を付けて発行してもらうよう依頼してください。  
  
## 📄 初回申請用：派遣状テンプレート  
新規で在留資格認定証明書（CoE）を申請する際に、海外の派遣元組織へ作成を依頼するテンプレートです。 

```text
[Official Letterhead of Sending Organization]

Date: [Day, Month, Year]

To Whom It May Concern:

This letter is to certify that [Full Legal Name as in Passport], born on [Date of Birth], holder of passport number [Passport Number], is a duly recognized [missionary / minister / pastor / religious worker / church worker] of [Name of Sending Organization].

[Name of Sending Organization] is a religious organization located at [full address] in [country]. Our organization is affiliated with [denomination / church network / mission body, if applicable] and is engaged in [brief description of religious activities and mission].

We hereby officially dispatch [Name] to Japan for the purpose of engaging in religious activities with Youth With A Mission Japan, a religious organization located in Japan.

1. Period of Dispatch
Expected duration: [e.g. 1 year / 3 years]

2. Position and Religious Status
During this assignment, [Name] will serve as a missionary.

[Name] has been recognized by our organization as follows:
Status: [ordained minister / commissioned missionary / appointed religious worker / etc.]
Date of [ordination / commissioning / appointment]: [date]
Summary of ministry background: [brief summary of ministry experience, training, or service]

3. Purpose and Nature of Activities in Japan
The purpose of this dispatch is for [Name] to engage in religious activities in Japan in cooperation with Youth With A Mission Japan.

The planned activities include, but are not limited to:
- conducting or assisting with worship services
- preaching and teaching
- Bible study or religious education
- discipleship and pastoral support
- prayer ministry
- outreach / evangelistic ministry
- support of the religious mission and activities of the receiving organization in Japan

These activities are religious in nature and are to be carried out as part of the mission of our organization and in cooperation with the receiving religious organization in Japan.

4. Financial Support / Remuneration
[Name] will not receive a salary from the receiving organization in Japan.

Instead, [Name]'s living expenses during the assignment in Japan will be covered through an established missionary support arrangement raised outside Japan. This support consists of contributions from individual and church supporters and is provided directly to [Name] rather than being processed through the receiving organization in Japan.

Our organization is fully aware of and endorses this support arrangement. We confirm that this is the recognized basis on which [Name] is being sent to carry out religious activities in Japan.

The approximate amount of financial support available to [Name] is [amount and currency] per month / [amount and currency] per year, which is intended to cover living expenses during the assignment in Japan.

5. Relationship to the Sending Organization
[Name] is being sent by [Name of Sending Organization] as part of our religious mission and remains under our [recognition / appointment / commissioning] during the assignment in Japan.

We respectfully request favorable consideration of [Name]'s application for a Certificate of Eligibility under the status of residence of Religious Activities.

If further information is required, please feel free to contact us.

Sincerely,

[Signature]
[Name of Signatory]
[Title / Position]
[Name of Sending Organization]
[Address]
[Telephone]
[Email]
```

## 📄 更新申請用：継続派遣状テンプレート  
すでに日本に滞在していて、現在の宗教ビザの在留期間更新を申請する際に使用するテンプレートです。 

```text
[Official Letterhead of Sending Organization]

Date: [Day, Month, Year]

To Whom It May Concern:

This letter is to certify that [Full Legal Name as in Passport], born on [Date of Birth], holder of passport number [Passport Number], remains a duly recognized [missionary / minister / pastor / religious worker / church worker] of [Name of Sending Organization].

[Name of Sending Organization] is a religious organization located at [full address] in [country]. Our organization is affiliated with [denomination / church network / mission body, if applicable] and is engaged in [brief description of religious activities and mission].

We confirm that [Name]'s assignment to Japan is continuing in cooperation with Youth With A Mission Japan, and that [Name] continues to be sent by our organization for the purpose of engaging in religious activities in Japan.

1. Continued Assignment / Period
Expected duration: [e.g. 1 year / 3 years]

2. Position and Religious Status
During this continued assignment, [Name] will continue to serve as a missionary.

[Name] remains under the [recognition / appointment / commissioning / ordination] of our organization.

3. Purpose and Nature of Activities in Japan
During the continued assignment in Japan, [Name] is expected to continue engaging in religious activities in cooperation with Youth With A Mission Japan.

These activities include, but are not limited to:
- conducting or assisting with worship services
- preaching and teaching
- Bible study or religious education
- discipleship and pastoral support
- prayer ministry
- outreach / evangelistic ministry
- support of the religious mission and activities of the receiving organization in Japan

4. Financial Support / Remuneration
[Name] will not receive a salary from the receiving organization in Japan.

Instead, [Name]'s living expenses during the continued assignment in Japan will be covered through an established missionary support arrangement raised outside Japan. This support consists of contributions from individual and church supporters and is provided directly to [Name] rather than being processed through the receiving organization in Japan.

Our organization is fully aware of and endorses this support arrangement. We confirm that [Name] continues to have financial support for living expenses during the assignment in Japan.

The approximate amount of financial support is [amount and currency] per month.

5. Organizational Endorsement
We confirm that [Name] continues to serve in Japan as part of the religious mission of our organization and remains under our recognition and endorsement during this assignment.

We respectfully request favorable consideration of [Name]'s application for Extension of Period of Stay under the status of residence of Religious Activities.

If further information is required, please feel free to contact us.

Sincerely,

[Signature]
[Name of Signatory]
[Title / Position]
[Name of Sending Organization]
[Address]
[Telephone]
[Email]
```
