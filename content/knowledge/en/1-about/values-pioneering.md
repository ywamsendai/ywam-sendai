## 🛠️ The Global Operating Culture
The YWAM Foundational Values define our core operating convictions, decision-making frameworks, and relational culture. These values differentiate our pioneering missionary environment from standard corporate or institutional systems.

## 📌 Values 1 through 9

### 1. Know God
> **In Brief:** We are committed to knowing God's nature, character, and ways as revealed in Scripture, letting our ministry overflow from a genuine relationship with Him.
* *Scripture:* Jer 9:23-24; Joh 17:3; Php 3:7-11

### 2. Make God Known
> **In Brief:** We are called to introduce God to every arena of society through evangelism, training, and mercy ministries, aiming for the holistic transformation of nations.
* *Scripture:* Mat 28:18-20; Mar 16:15; Act 1:8

### 3. Hear God's Voice
> **In Brief:** We depend on hearing God's voice as individuals and together in teams for corporate discernment and everyday decision-making.
* *Scripture:* Joh 10:1-5; Joh 16:13-15; Heb 3:7-8

### 4. Practice Worship & Intercessory Prayer
> **In Brief:** We embed corporate worship and prayer into our daily schedules as essential weapons to overcome spiritual opposition and advance the mission.
* *Scripture:* Psa 100:1-5; Eph 6:13-20; 1Th 5:16-19

### 5. Be Visionary
> **In Brief:** We actively pursue and release fresh, radical methods and ministries from God to remain relevant to shifting generations and cultures.
* *Scripture:* Pro 29:18; Hab 2:2-3; Act 16:9-10

### 6. Champion Young People
> **In Brief:** We choose to value, trust, train, and release youth to spearhead vision and leadership today, committing to support them as full members of the Church.
* *Scripture:* Jer 1:5-10; 1Ti 4:12-16; 1Jo 2:12-14

### 7. Be Decentralized
> **In Brief:** We operate as a global volunteer movement without a centralized administrative hierarchy, leaving spiritual responsibility and authority with local bases.
* *Scripture:* Exo 18:13-26; Act 15:1-31; Tit 1:5-9

### 8. Be International & Interdenominational
> **In Brief:** We celebrate ethnic, linguistic, and denominational diversity as vital components that enhance the health and reach of the mission.
* *Scripture:* Gen 12:1-4; Act 20:4; Col 3:11; Rev 7:9

### 9. Have a Biblical Christian Worldview
> **In Brief:** We view the Bible as the textbook for all of life, recognizing that ordinary, practical work is just as spiritual as explicit ministry expressions.
* *Scripture:* Psa 19:7-11; 2Ti 3:16-17; Jam 4:17
