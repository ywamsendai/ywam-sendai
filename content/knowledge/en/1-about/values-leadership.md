## 📌 Values 10 through 18

### 10. Function in Teams
> **In Brief:** We execute all leadership and ministry within a team framework, relying on a mix of gifts and viewpoints for safety, accountability, and wisdom.
* *Scripture:* Pro 15:22; Ecc 4:9-12; Rom 12:3-10

### 11. Exhibit Servant Leadership
> **In Brief:** We choose leadership as a lifestyle of service rather than institutional hierarchy, actively guarding the growth and rights of those under our care.
* *Scripture:* Mar 10:42-45; Joh 13:3-17; Php 2:3-11

### 12. Do First, Then Teach
> **In Brief:** We believe firsthand experience gives authority to our words, prioritizing proven godly character and lifestyle over abstract technical expertise.
* *Scripture:* Ezr 7:10; Mat 7:28-29; Act 1:1-2

### 13. Be Relationship-Oriented
> **In Brief:** We build community through holiness, deep transparency, and mutual support, intentionally relying on real relationships over rigid rulebooks.
* *Scripture:* Joh 13:34-35; Joh 17:20-23; 1Jo 1:7

### 14. Value the Individual
> **In Brief:** We advocate for equal opportunity and justice, honoring the unique callings and contributions of both men and women across all generations.
* *Scripture:* Gen 1:27; Psa 139:13-16; Gal 3:28

### 15. Value Families
> **In Brief:** We encourage families and single parents to serve together in missions, affirming the biblical standard of holy marriage between one man and one woman.
* *Scripture:* Gen 2:21-24; Pro 31:10-31; Heb 13:4
 
### 16. Practice Dependence on God
> **In Brief:** We operate on a financial self-support model, depending entirely on God's provision through his people, and practicing radical generosity in return.
* *Scripture:* Mat 6:25-33; 2Co 8:1-9:15; Php 4:10-20

### 17. Practice Hospitality
> **In Brief:** We open our homes, hearts, and base facilities to serve guests, teammates, and the vulnerable as a genuine expression of God's generous character.
* *Scripture:* Mat 25:31-46; Rom 12:13; Heb 13:1-3

### 18. Communicate with Integrity
> **In Brief:** We commit to truthful, accurate, clear, and timely communication across all organizational levels to maintain trust and community health.
* *Scripture:* Pro 25:9-14; Mat 5:33-37; Col 4:6; Jam 3:1-18
