## 🎓 Academic Standards
Students must participate fully in the academic life of their school. This covers classroom lectures, assigned readings, written reflections, and outreach prep. Grading is recorded via University of the Nations outcomes: **Successful (S) or Unsuccessful (U)**. Repeat absences or persistent disengagement will lead to an Unsuccessful track completion and loss of credit.

## ⏰ Attendance Rules
Attendance across all scheduled lectures, base activities, and intercession slots is non-negotiable. If you are sick or physically unable to attend a session, you must notify your school leader immediately **before the activity starts**.

## 💡 Learning Support
If you struggle with reading comprehension or written assignments, communicate early. We provide practical guidance, language support, and adjusted timelines to help you cross the finish line successfully.

## 🧹 Work Duties & Facility Care
Practical work duties are an integrated element of character discipleship. Students are assigned to daily operational tasks, including deep cleaning, kitchen responsibilities, and general base maintenance. You are responsible for keeping your shared living quarters pristine.

## 🌙 Romantic Boundaries
Students are required to refrain from pursuing or starting new romantic relationships for the duration of the school. This standard maximizes your emotional focus on God and core community formation.

## ⚖️ Restoration & Disciplinary Steps
Our disciplinary framework aims for pastoral restoration rather than punishment. When serious behavioral, community, or academic violations occur, we utilize a 4-staged escalation process:

### 💬 1. Pastoral Encouragement
An informal, direct feedback and alignment chat with your school staff to address the initial heart issue.

### 📝 2. Written Warning
A formal, documented warning outlining required behavioral changes and clear character expectations.

### ⏳ 3. Final Warning
A fixed trial window with highly specific, measurable metrics for survival in the training school.

### 🚪 4. Dismissal
Official removal from the school without academic credit. Students are sent home at their own expense.

> 🛑 **Summary Dismissal:** YWAM Sendai reserves the right to execute immediate dismissal without walking through the gradual warning stages if a student's behavior poses an active safety threat, severely damages the community fabric, or demonstrates a proud, persistent refusal to accept accountability.
