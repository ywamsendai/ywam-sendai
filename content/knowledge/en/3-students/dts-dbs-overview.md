## 🎓 The Live-Learn Training Posture
Training at YWAM Sendai integrates intense spiritual formation, deep biblical foundations, community living, and cross-cultural mission. Our schools are immersive, live-learn environments designed to develop inner character, spiritual intimacy, and real-world ministry readiness.

## 🇯🇵 Fully Bilingual Operations
All training tracks aim to operate simultaneously in English and Japanese. To accommodate this rich cross-cultural learning model, our tracks require a high level of daily focus:
* **Total Weekly Training Volume:** Minimum of 50 total learning hours per week.
* **Direct Content Engagement:** Minimum of 16 hours of bilingual lecture and class time.

## 🏔️ Discipleship Training School (DTS)
The DTS is the foundational entry-level program of YWAM global. It serves as a strict prerequisite for all further University of the Nations (U of N) training and for long-term missionary staff service. The school lasts 5 to 6 months and is split into two mandatory phases.

## 📝 DTS Lecture Phase
The lecture phase utilizes classroom teaching, small-group processing, 1-on-1 mentoring, and personal reflection to shape interior character. Students participate in a minimum of 3 hours per week of corporate intercession and regular base worship.
* *Core Teaching:* Nature and Character of God, Hearing God's Voice, Father Heart of God, Identity in Christ, Sin & Redemption, and the Kingdom of God.

## 🗺️ DTS Outreach Phase
A team-based deployment putting discipleship into practice in cross-cultural or local ministry settings. Focuses on evangelism, mercy ministry, church partnerships, and practical service.
* *Weekly Workload:* Minimum of 30 hours of outward ministry and 15 hours of team building, prayer, and debriefing.

## 📖 Discipleship Bible School (DBS)
The DBS is an intensive 12-week track focused on deep, firsthand engagement with the Word of God. Students read completely through the entire Bible chronologically from Genesis to Revelation, discovering the text as one unified story of redemption.
* **Prerequisite:** Successful completion of a YWAM DTS.

## 🎯 DBS Inductive Approach
DBS equips students to handle the Word of God with integrity. It utilizes an inductive approach—teaching students to observe text carefully, map historical and literary context, recognize authorial intent, and isolate original meaning from personal bias.
