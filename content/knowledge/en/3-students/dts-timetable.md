## 🌅 Morning Devotional Rhythms
Your daily community schedule triggers early to prioritize personal spiritual focus:
* **07:00:** Breakfast is served daily.
* **07:30 - 08:30:** Mandatory personal Quiet Time.

## 🛐 Corporate Base Prayer Tracks
Every morning at 08:30, the community gathers corporately for prayer and worship:
* **Mondays & Wednesdays:** Intense corporate Worship.
* **Tuesdays & Fridays:** Strategic corporate Intercession.

## 🗺️ Thursday Specialized Intercession
Every Thursday morning from 08:30 to 09:30, the school pivots to targeted intercession. This slot alternates between Unreached Unengaged People Groups (UUPGs), specialized global ministries, and territorial prayer for the six prefectures of the Tohoku region.

## 🏫 Lecture & Processing Blocks
Primary classroom teaching runs Monday through Friday:
* **09:30 - 10:45:** Lecture Block 1.
* **10:45 - 11:00:** Community Rest Break.
* **11:00 - 12:15:** Lecture Block 2.
* **12:15 - 12:30:** Mandatory Processing & Q&A Window.
* **12:30:** Lunch and base cleanup.

## 🏃 Afternoon Ministry & Training
Afternoon tracks focus on practical application and research:
* **Mondays:** Tohoku Project research and development.
* **Tuesdays & Thursdays:** Local outreach deployments, personal staff one-on-ones, and book research.
* **Wednesdays:** Japanese language and culture classes with direct outreach prep.

## 🧹 Daily Discipleship Work Duties
From 16:00 to 17:30 on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, all students join operational base work duties. This covers deep kitchen cleaning, facility maintenance, and administrative tasks. Dinner and cleanup follow from 17:30 to 19:00.

## 🌙 Evening Community Tracking
Evening tracks begin at 19:00:
* **Mondays:** Small groups or organized community social nights.
* **Wednesdays:** City-wide prayer walks and direct street evangelism.
* **Thursdays:** Public Community Worship & Word night.
* **22:00:** Strict quiet hours activate across all housing units.
