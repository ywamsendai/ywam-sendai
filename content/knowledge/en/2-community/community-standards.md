## 🕊️ Our Shared Commitments
Life at YWAM Sendai is built on a shared blueprint of spiritual, relational, and practical commitments. These standards protect our collective health, keeping us grounded and accountable. They apply equally to long-term staff, students, outreach teams, interns, and short-term visitors. Choosing to join this community means actively engaging with this framework.

## 🎐 The Posture of a Learner
Staff members, students, and short-term participants enter a mature, existing cross-cultural missionary community. All are expected to adapt respectfully to our established operational rhythms rather than demanding that the base alter its schedules or habits around them. We require guests to arrive with a posture of listening before speaking, and learning before evaluating.

---

## ⛪ Core Behavioral & Spiritual Baselines

### 🩸 Spiritual Grounding
We expect everyone in our community to anchor their day-to-day lives in an authentic relationship with Jesus. This looks like active participation in corporate worship, base prayer tracks, regular Scripture reflection, and personal teachability. We focus entirely on inner transformation and obedience rather than empty religious performance.

### 🤝 Relational Integrity & Speech
A healthy, cross-cultural missionary community requires taking absolute personal ownership of your speech and attitudes. We expect members to interact with honesty, humility, and love:
* **Direct Communication:** Resolve interpersonal conflicts directly and responsibly with the person involved, completely avoiding gossip, triangulation, and passive-aggressive behavior.
* **Mutual Respect:** Intentionally honor personal and cultural boundaries across different nationalities and generations.

### 💍 Marriage, Sexuality & Gender
All staff, students, teams, and visitors are required to respect our ministry's historical Christian convictions. In alignment with our theological framework and our vital partnership with local Japanese churches:
* **Marriage:** We recognize marriage exclusively as a lifelong covenant union between one man and one woman.
* **Sexuality:** We believe sexual expression belongs strictly within this sacred marital covenant.
* **Gender:** We expect individuals to live in complete alignment with their biological sex.

### 🛠️ Work as Discipleship
Ordinary chores and administrative logistics are core elements of personal growth, not distractions from "real" ministry. Depending on your current track or staff role, your operational assignments will include school staffing, base administration, outreach prep, facility deep-cleaning, property maintenance, hospitality hosting, and peer mentoring.

---

## 🛑 Relational Boundaries & Intervention

### 📦 Constrained Housing Discipline
Living space inside urban Japan is highly constrained and structurally limited. Staff, students, and short-term teams must be prepared for close, high-density shared living conditions. We require strict personal discipline regarding cleanliness, prompt completion of daily chores, absolute respect for designated quiet hours, and careful stewardship of communal utilities (water and electricity).

### ❌ Prohibited & Harmful Behaviors
We maintain a zero-tolerance baseline for conduct that damages individual safety or community trust. Explicitly harmful behaviors will spark an immediate disciplinary review and include:
* Dishonesty, theft, or property damage.
* Harassment, intimidation, aggression, or verbal abuse.
* Sexual misconduct or relational manipulation.
* Persistent defiance of base leadership or standards.
* **Substance Policy:** All staff, students, and visitors are required to completely abstain from smoking, vaping, and alcohol intoxication during their time with YWAM Sendai.

### ⚠️ Accountability & Dismissal
We do not use these community standards as a legalistic rulebook, but as a definition of our cultural boundaries. If an applicant or participant cannot sincerely align with these standards, they must communicate this honestly with leadership before arrival. 
> 🛑 **Immediate Intervention:** If a staff member, student, or short-term visitor breaches these boundaries, leadership will intervene instantly. Persistent mismatch or serious safety violations will result in a formal disciplinary track or the premature termination of your stay at your own personal expense.
