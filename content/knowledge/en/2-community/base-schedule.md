## 🗓️ The Purpose of Shared Rhythms
We anchor our community life in structured, recurring weekly rhythms rather than constant improvisation. Clear patterns create a sustainable, predictable framework for worship, work, hospitality, rest, and language learning. Without these common boundaries, missionary life quickly becomes fragmented, reactive, and exhausting. Rhythms keep our community sustainable for the long haul.

---

## 📅 The Weekly Base Calendar

### 🛐 Monday Corporate Tracks
* **10:00 - 12:00 | Worship & Intercession:** The spiritual engine room of our week. We gather as a whole community for corporate worship, strategic prayer, and listening to God.
  * *Location:* Faith Christian Fellowship Church (FCBC) in central Sendai.
* **13:30 - 14:30 | Staff Meeting (Staff Only):** Internal alignment track. We share real-time testimonies, receive operational updates from ministry departments, and map out upcoming logisitics.
  * *Location:* Ayashi base space.

### 👥 Scheduled Mid-Week Tracks
* **Wednesday Evening | Kokubuncho Outreach:** Light in the entertainment district. We gather for corporate worship, strategic prayer walking, and street evangelism in the central parks of *Kokubuncho*, Sendai's red-light district.
* **Friday Morning | Homeless Ministry Partnership:** Hands-on urban mercy. We partner directly with a local Sendai church to serve, feed, and care for the city's unhoused population.

### 🥖 Monthly & Occasional Tracks
* **The Common Table (Once a Month):** A vital pillar of discipleship and cross-cultural trust. We prepare and share a massive meal together in one of our community homes to celebrate birthdays, practice bilingual conversation, and extend intentional hospitality to local neighborhood guests.
* **Monthly Kotodai Park Event:** Mobile kitchen outreach. We deploy our base kitchen car to a monthly feeding and community event in Kotodai Park (central Sendai) to support the unhoused.
* **Japanese Practice Sessions:** Spontaneous and scheduled interactive meetups designed to practice conversational Japanese through games and peer-to-peer sharing. 

### ⚡ Weekend Boundaries
* **Saturday (Full Sabbath):** Dedicated entirely to personal recovery, digital boundaries, unplugging, and deep physical rest. No base meetings or ministries are scheduled.
* **Sunday (Local Church Honor):** Active participation, corporate worship, and volunteer service within local Japanese churches across the Tohoku region.

## 🏔️ Active Ministry Platforms

### 🎓 Student Ministry
We regularly attend and host exchange events at local Sendai universities. This serves as a strategic platform for natural language exchange, cultural sharing, and building deep, relational bridges with Japanese students.

### 🚶‍♂️ Prayer Walks
We conduct strategic prayer walks across diverse neighborhoods, high-traffic train stations, commercial shopping districts, universities, local shrines, and temples. We observe the spiritual atmosphere, listen for God's heart, and actively seek opportunities to seed new ministries and secure a dedicated permanent facility for our base operations.
