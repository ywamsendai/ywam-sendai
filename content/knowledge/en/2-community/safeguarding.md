## 🛡️ Zero Tolerance for Abuse
YWAM Sendai maintains an absolute, zero-tolerance policy regarding abuse, exploitation, spiritual coercion, and harassment of any kind. Safeguarding is the foundational bedrock of our community trust. Protecting children, vulnerable adults, and those in dependent training situations is a binding Christian responsibility that completely overrides individual privacy or institutional reputation.

## ⚠️ Identifying Safeguarding Risks
A safeguarding concern or violation includes, but is not limited to:
* Sexual misconduct, inappropriate touch, or harassment.
* Physical violence or emotional abuse.
* Spiritual manipulation, unchecked power imbalances, or coercive control.
* Inappropriate or unmonitored private 1-on-1 settings involving minors or students.
* Any persistent behavior that intimidates, exploits, or creates a culture of fear.

## 📢 Immediate Reporting Procedure
If you witness, experience, or suspect unsafe conduct, you must report it immediately to a designated safeguarding contact or base leadership. Do not treat serious behavioral red flags or safety violations as private matters to be managed through informal chats. 
* *Immediate Danger:* If an individual is in immediate physical danger, prioritize emergency services (110 or 119) above all internal base protocols.

## 🔐 The Limits of Confidentiality
While we handle all safety concerns with deep care and emotional sensitivity, confidentiality is never secrecy. Information regarding harm, abuse, or risk will be shared immediately with the necessary legal authorities and global leadership to ensure safety and secure appropriate justice. We will always prioritize the safety of the vulnerable over the preservation of our organizational image.
