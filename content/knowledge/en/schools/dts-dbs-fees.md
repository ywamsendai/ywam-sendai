## 💰 Financial Responsibility
Students are fully responsible for tracking and clearing their school costs on time. Personal flights to/from Japan, mandatory health insurance, personal SIM data plans, clothing, toiletries, and independent free-time expenses are entirely your responsibility.

## 💳 Milestone 1: Pre-Arrival Transfers
Do not assume payments can be delayed until arrival. We enforce a strict digital transfer schedule:
1. **Enrollment Deposit (¥100,000):** Due immediately upon formal acceptance to lock your spot.
2. **Tuition Balance (¥200,000):** Due exactly 10 days before your school's official start date.

## 💴 Milestone 2: On-Site Cash Payments
Because Japan operates on a cash-heavy infrastructure, specific core fees must be handed over physically on site in Japanese Yen (JPY):
3. **Accommodation Fee (¥120,000):** Paid in full via physical JPY cash during Registration Day.
4. **Outreach Payment (DTS Only - Approx ¥400,000):** Paid in full via physical JPY cash 1 month before outreach begins.

## 🛑 General Refund Principles
If you must withdraw, cancel, or leave a school early, notify leadership instantly. Tuition fees are generally non-refundable once class lectures begin. Accommodation fees are refunded on a strict, pro-rated weekly scale based on your actual physical departure date.

## ✈️ Outreach Fee Refunds
Outreach fees are spent far in advance to book team flights and housing. Withdrawal prior to outreach may be refunded *minus* airline cancellation penalties and funds already legally committed. Withdrawal during outreach is entirely non-refundable due to sunk logistical costs.
