## 📖 The Biblical Reference Point
Youth With A Mission affirms the Bible as the inspired and authoritative Word of God and the absolute reference point for every single aspect of life, community development, and cross-cultural ministry. Based upon God’s Word and His initiative of salvation through the atoning work of Jesus, the movement strongly emphasizes six specific behavioral and spiritual responses.

## 🏛️ Six Emphasized Structural Responses

### 1. Worship & Praise
> **In Brief:** We are called to praise and worship God alone, completely rejecting modern idolatry, syncretism, and rival loyalties.
* *Key Passages:* Exo 20:2-3; Deu 6:4-5; Mar 12:29-30; Rev 5:6-14

### 2. Personal Holiness
> **In Brief:** We are called to lead holy, righteous, and disciplined lives that actively reflect and exhibit the character of God.
* *Key Passages:* Lev 19:1-2; Eph 4:21-32; Tit 2:11-14; 1Pe 2:9

### 3. Active Witness
> **In Brief:** We are called to clearly, boldly, and contextually share the Gospel of Jesus Christ with those who do not know Him.
* *Key Passages:* Luk 24:44-48; Act 1:8; Act 10:39-43; 1Pe 3:15-18

### 4. Intercessory Prayer
> **In Brief:** We are called to engage in consistent prayer for nations, individuals, and structural causes on God’s heart, actively standing against spiritual and systemic evil.
* *Key Passages:* Eze 22:30-31; Mat 6:5-15; Eph 6:13-20

### 5. Christian Fellowship
> **In Brief:** We are called to commit deeply to the Church in both its local nurturing expression and its mobile, multiplying missionary expression.
* *Key Passages:* Act 2:44-47; Eph 2:11-18; Heb 10:23-25

### 6. Sacrificial Service
> **In Brief:** We are called to contribute practically toward God’s Kingdom purposes across every cultural sphere and everyday dimension of life.
* *Key Passages:* Mat 5:14-16; Tit 3:4-8; Jam 2:14-26

## 🎯 Application to Sendai Rhythms
These core responses form the absolute baseline for all local community rhythms in Sendai. They directly dictate how our training school lecture schedules are constructed, how our corporate prayer tracks are formed, and how staff members execute everyday service, ensuring local base operations stay aligned with historic biblical truth.
