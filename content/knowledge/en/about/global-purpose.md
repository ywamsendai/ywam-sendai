## 🌍 Global Movement Identity
Youth With A Mission (YWAM) is an international, faith-based movement of Christians from many denominations. The global body is collectively dedicated to presenting Jesus Christ personally to this and future generations, mobilizing as many individuals as possible to assist in this objective, and training believers to execute their specific roles within the fulfillment of the Great Commission.

## 👑 Kingdom Identity & Scope
As citizens of God’s kingdom, global members are called to love, worship, and obey the Lord, to love and serve His body (the Church), and to love all peoples everywhere. This movement-wide mandate requires presenting the whole Gospel for the whole person throughout the whole world, refusing to let structural, cultural, or national boundaries limit missional deployment.

## 📜 Doctrinal Foundations
The movement's purpose is anchored in structural, orthodox Christian convictions:
* **The Triune God:** Absolute belief in God the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
* **Scriptural Authority:** Affirming the Bible as God’s inspired, authoritative Word.
* **The Atoning Work of Christ:** Salvation is made possible exclusively through the incarnation, sinless life, death, resurrection, and ascension of Jesus Christ.
* **Human Response:** Repentance, active faith, unconditional love, and structural obedience are the vital responses to God’s initiative of grace through the Holy Spirit.

## 🎌 Local Expression in Sendai
YWAM Sendai functions as a direct local expression of this global movement rather than an isolated, independent ministry. This master framework is practically applied across the Tohoku region through focused discipleship schools, local church partnerships, relational hospitality, and a long-term commitment to helping individuals know God and make Him known.
