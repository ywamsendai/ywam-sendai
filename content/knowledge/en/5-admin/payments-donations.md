## 💳 Base Payment Channels
YWAM Sendai processes all inbound corporate fees, staff contributions, and ministry donations through designated channels based on your geographic location. Wise is our required standard for all international digital transfers, while standard domestic bank transfers (Furikomi) are utilized inside Japan. We do not accept direct international wire transfers due to heavy processing overheads.

## 🗺️ International Transfers via Wise
For students, staff, and donors transferring funds from outside Japan, Wise is the mandatory standard. Wise converts your local currency into Japanese yen before the transaction hits our system, ensuring significantly lower fees and optimal exchange rates. When configuring an international Wise transfer, you must route the funds exclusively to our GMO Aozora Net Bank account.

## 💴 Cash Requirements in Japan
Japan's local infrastructure remains heavily dependent on physical currency. Certain on-site operational costs—specifically base accommodation fees, food budgets, and specialized school outreach expenses—must be paid physically on-site using Japanese Yen cash. Follow all track-specific or leadership directives regarding cash-only milestones explicitly.

## 🏧 ATM Operations & Cash Planning
Plan your physical currency acquisition well in advance of base payment deadlines to avoid transaction blocks. Use international-friendly ATM networks like 7-Bank (located inside all 7-Eleven convenience stores) or Japan Post Bank ATMs to withdraw local currency. Because daily international limits vary, execute your total required withdrawals across multiple days.

## 📧 Mandatory Transfer Notifications
Whenever you send an international or domestic digital transaction, you must file an immediate notification. Digital banking records do not clearly display donor or student names, causing severe processing bottlenecks. Send a confirmation email instantly to **`finance@ywamsendai.org`** using the exact subject line: *Payment from [Your Name]*.

## 📋 Notification Content Template
Copy, populate, and transmit this precise information block within your confirmation email to our finance team:
* **Full Name:** [Your Name]
* **Date Sent:** [YYYY-MM-DD]
* **Amount & Currency:** [e.g., 300,000 JPY]
* **Payment Method Used:** [e.g., Wise / Domestic Transfer / Stripe]
* **Purpose of Payment:** [e.g., DTS Tuition Deposit / Staff Fees / General Donation / Staff Support]
* **Notes:** [e.g., Designated project name, or entry error tracking notes]

## 🎁 Supporter Giving Posture
YWAM Sendai depends on God’s provision through the continuous financial generosity of partners. Because we are a voluntary missionary movement, external financial donations directly sustain our localized ministries, outreach programs, training tracks, base hospitality infrastructures, and community operations across the Tohoku region.

## 💸 Donation Classification
Supporters can coordinate with our base finance team to allocate funding across several clear streams:
* **General Donations:** Unrestricted funds deployed dynamically to base operating costs and immediate infrastructure needs.
* **Staff Support:** Targeted funds routed securely to sustain specific long-term missionary staff.
* **Project Support:** Contributions reserved strictly for specified local ministries, initiatives, or seasonal outreaches.
