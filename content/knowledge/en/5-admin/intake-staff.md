## 🚪 Long-Term Staff Application
Staff placement at YWAM Sendai is processed through our centralized digital portal. Joining the pioneer missionary community requires deep flexibility and spiritual alignment. Every staff application is evaluated against character references and base operational capacity.

## 🪪 Staff Eligibility Prerequisites
To apply for a long-term missionary position on the base, you must meet these non-negotiable requirements:
* **DTS Graduation:** You must have successfully completed a UofN verified Discipleship Training School.
* **Linguistic Skill:** You must demonstrate functional independent proficiency in English or Japanese.

## 📥 Staff Intake Flow: Steps 1 to 3
* **Step 1: Initial Submission:** Submit the master form online and explicitly select the long-term staff track.
* **Step 2: Reference Screening:** Our team audits your pastoral, leadership, and peer character references.
* **Step 3: Leadership Interview:** Engage in a video call with base leadership to discuss ministry calling and local expectations.

## ✉️ Staff Placement Flow: Steps 4 to 7
* **Step 4: Formal Invitation:** If leadership alignment is solid, you receive an official staff acceptance notification.
* **Step 5: Budget Lockdown:** Work with the finance office to establish your personal funding benchmarks and clear financial milestones.
* **Step 6: CoE Activation:** Once funding targets are fully met, the base triggers your legal Certificate of Eligibility paperwork.
* **Step 7: Civic Registration:** Land in Sendai, report to the ward office for local registration, and start staff orientation.

## 🛑 Strict Flight Booking Rule
Incoming staff must not book airline tickets or execute irreversible relocation plans until your physical visa sticker is secured in your passport. CoE processing takes 6 to 12 weeks. You must legally enter Japan within 3 months of CoE issuance, or it becomes void.
