## 🛃 Legal Stay Responsibility
All non-Japanese long-term staff members are personally and legally responsible for maintaining valid immigration status while living in Japan. YWAM Sendai serves as your supporting organization and provides administrative guidance, but final entry approvals belong exclusively to the Ministry of Justice. Never treat immigration details casually.

## 🛫 Financial Readiness Lock
YWAM Sendai will not trigger your Certificate of Eligibility (CoE) application with Japanese Immigration until you have officially cleared your financial readiness benchmarks. Staff are legally prohibited from arriving on tourist waivers with the assumption that legal status can be fixed later. Your support system must be fully locked down before any paperwork is filed.

## 📑 CoE Required Documents
To process an initial Religious Activities visa CoE, send clear digital scans to `people@ywamsendai.org` and mail the physical copies. Required items:
* High-resolution color scan of your passport identity page.
* A professional passport portrait file.
* Verified theological certificates (such as your DTS certificate).
* An official, signed Dispatch Letter from your sending church.

## ⛪ The Sending Church Dispatch Letter
Our standardized [church letter template](visa-templates.md) states that your sending organization provides financial support during your term. This assurance is a critical legal check for Japanese Immigration to ensure you do not become destitute. This letter is not a legally binding contract forcing your church to meet every need; it functions as an official organizational voucher.

## 📸 Portrait Quality Baselines
Japanese immigration strictly rejects incorrect portrait files. Your submittal must meet these criteria: exact dimensions of 4cm height by 3cm width, taken within the last 6 months against a plain, solid background. It must be a front-facing profile with a neutral expression, clear focus, no shadow patterns, and absolutely no hats or headwear.

## 🛑 Strict Flight Booking Rule
Do not book airline tickets or execute irreversible relocation plans until your physical visa sticker is firmly secured inside your passport. CoE processing takes 6 to 12 weeks and fluctuates wildly based on backlogs. Note: You must legally enter Japan within 3 months of your CoE's date of issue, or the document becomes entirely void.

## 🔄 Visa Renewal Landmarks
If you are already on staff in Japan, you can legally initiate your Extension of Period of Stay process starting exactly 3 months prior to your current visa's expiration date. To begin, message our compliance officer your current Residence Card details, your desired extension length (1, 3, or 5 years), and the official name and address of your sending church.

## 📄 Renewal Document Checklist
To apply for your visa extension at the Sendai Immigration office, you must compile: a completed application form (including Page 3 from YWAM Sendai), your passport, your current residence card, a fresh 4cm x 3cm portrait photo, a YWAM Japan Invitation/Warranty, a new Church Dispatch Letter, and a Japanese Tax or Tax Exemption Certificate.

## 🏛️ Local Tax Certificates for Renewal
Your mandatory Japanese Tax (or Tax Exemption) Certificate must be issued directly by the local city office or ward office where you legally resided as of January 1st of the previous year. If you moved to Sendai recently, you must request this document from your previous municipality. This paper is vital to prove your local civic compliance.

## ✉️ Application Processing & Postcard Pickup
After submitting your renewal documents at the Sendai Immigration office, processing typically takes between 2 weeks and 1 month. You will receive an official notification postcard in your mail. Take this postcard, your passport, your current residence card, and a ¥6,000 revenue stamp (purchased at a post office) back to the office to collect your new card.
