## 🚗 Driving Legally in Miyagi Prefecture
If you intend to operate vehicles during your missional term, you should convert your foreign driver's license to a Japanese license (*Gaikoku Menkyo Kirikae*). The official legal protocol for our region is managed directly via the Miyagi Prefectural Police Department. 

## 📝 The JAF Official Translation
Before booking an evaluation appointment with the licensing center, you must secure an official, legally recognized Japanese translation of your foreign driver's license. This document must be processed through the Japan Automobile Federation (**JAF**). Follow the digital ordering frameworks detailed explicitly on the official JAF web portal.

## 🛂 Proving the 3-Month Residency Rule
To qualify for a license transfer, you must prove legally that you resided in your issuing country for a minimum of **3 consecutive months** after your foreign license was first issued. While a physical passport stamp trail is the standard validation method, modern automated immigration gates mean you may need alternate corroborating evidence.

## 📄 Alternate Residency Documentation
If your current passport lacks physical arrival/departure stamps or was recently renewed, compile alternative official records to verify your 3-month residency window since your first license issue date. Acceptable records include university graduation certificates, official tax documents, or consecutive credit card receipts. **Tip:** Providing 4 to 5 months of documentation is ideal; compiling over a full year of proof allows you to drive without displaying a beginner's magnet mark.

## 📄 The Ward Certificate (Jūminhyō)
You must submit a physical Certificate of Residence (**Jūminhyō**) obtained directly from your local municipal ward office. When requesting this printout at the city service counter, you must explicitly instruct the clerk to include your **nationality** on the form. By default, the system redacts nationality details unless specifically requested, rendering the document invalid for license processing.

## 👁️ Licensing Center Testing Protocols
Depending on your country of citizenship, certain bilateral agreements exempt specific applicants from the practical driving test and written exam. Verify your country's status via the Tokyo Metropolitan Police multilingual conversion guides. On your physical testing day at the Miyagi license center, pass an essential visual acuity exam; ensure you bring corrective glasses or contact lenses if required.
